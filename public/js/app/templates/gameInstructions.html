Game Instructions

JEOPARDY!
is a simple Game where you have to choose a question and provide the right answer to it to gain the value of the question on top of your score.

Singleplayer:
Choose one of the five values from one of the five categories.
Read the question and enter your answer.
Click submit to see if your answer was correct.
Repeat with one of the remaining questions.

Multiplayer:
First you need to decide who will host the game and act like the gamemaster. The Gamemaster has to click the choosen question and read it out loud to the participants. For each given answer he has so select the player in the PopUp and then click if the player gave the correct or incorrect answer to add or subract the value of the question from this players score.
After that Player 1 begins to choose a question.
The Gamemaster reads the question to the players and the first player to give a sign is allowed to answer the question.
If the player answers the question correctly he is the player to choose the next question.
If the player answers the question incorrectly the other players have the possbility to answer the question. If nobody gets the right answer the player who has choosen this question is allowed to pick the next one.
